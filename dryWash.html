What is Dry Cleaning?

Dry cleaning is a professional cleaning method for delicate garments and fabrics like silk, wool, and formal attire. Instead of water, it uses chemical solvents to remove stains and dirt while preserving the texture, shape, and color of your clothes. Ideal for high-maintenance fabrics, it ensures a thorough clean without causing damage.

Why Should You Choose Dry Cleaning?

Dry cleaning protects your clothes from common issues like shrinkage, fading, and distortion caused by traditional washing methods. It effectively handles tough stains such as grease and oil, offering a superior clean for items like suits, dresses, and special fabrics. Moreover, it saves time and ensures your garments maintain their crisp, polished look.

How Does Dry Cleaning Work?

The process begins with inspecting your clothes to identify stains and cleaning needs. Stains are pre-treated with care, and garments are placed in a machine with a special solvent to remove dirt and oils. After cleaning, clothes are inspected again, steamed, and pressed to restore their original look. The final step ensures your items are returned in immaculate condition.
