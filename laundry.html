What is Laundry?

Laundry refers to the cleaning process of washing clothes, bedding, and other fabric items. It involves using water, detergent, and sometimes fabric softeners or bleach to remove dirt, stains, and odors from clothes. This process can be done at home or by using a laundry service.

Why Should You Choose Laundry?

Laundry is essential for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene. It removes bacteria, allergens, and dirt from clothes, helping to keep you healthy. Regular washing also ensures that clothes stay in good condition, preventing fabric deterioration and maintaining their appearance. For those with busy lifestyles or large households, laundry services offer convenience, saving time and effort.

How Does Laundry Work?

The laundry process begins by sorting clothes based on fabric type, color, and washing requirements. Clothes are placed into a washing machine, where they are cleaned with water and detergent. Once dry, clothes are folded or pressed to remove wrinkles, making them ready to wear again.
