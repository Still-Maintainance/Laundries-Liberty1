Privacy Policy for Smart Wash

At Smart Wash, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services, website, or mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Information You Provide Directly: Name, contact number, email address, delivery address, payment details, and preferences for laundry services.
  • Automatically Collected Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage details about interactions with our website or app.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Cookies to enhance your browsing experience and gather usage insights. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our laundry services.
  • To communicate with you about orders, updates, promotions, and support.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To analyze user behavior and improve functionality.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: For payment processing, delivery, or technical support.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect the rights of Smart Wash.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access to the information we hold about you.
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdrawal of consent for specific data uses.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

6. Retention of Data

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations.

7. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website or app may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing your information.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
